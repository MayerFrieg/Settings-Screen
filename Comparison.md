# Healthcare Practice Management Platforms: Comprehensive Comparison

## Executive Summary

This document provides a detailed comparison of four leading healthcare practice management platforms: **SimplePractice**, **Jane App**, **CarePatron**, and **Colib**. Each platform offers unique strengths tailored to different practice types, sizes, and geographic markets.

### Quick Overview

| Platform | Settings Count | Primary Market | Best For | Color Theme |
|----------|---------------|----------------|----------|-------------|
| **SimplePractice** | 28+ | USA Mental Health | Insurance billing, therapy practices | Blue (#3B82F6) |
| **Jane App** | 65+ | Canadian Multi-Disciplinary | Allied health, multi-specialty clinics | Teal (#14B8A6) |
| **CarePatron** | 30+ | Global Small Teams | Solo/small practices, workflow automation | Emerald (#0D9488) |
| **Colib** | 25+ | Canadian Bilingual | Bilingual practices, telehealth focus | Lime (#84CC16) |

## 1. SETTINGS ORGANIZATION & NAVIGATION

### SimplePractice
**Structure**: 3 main categories
- **Operations** (8 settings)
- **Billing** (7 settings)
- **Client Care** (13 settings)

**Navigation Style**:
- Fixed left sidebar with expandable sections
- Hierarchical menu structure
- Link-based page navigation
- Dashboard quick links (8 primary settings)

**Complexity**: ★★★☆☆ (Moderate - well-organized hierarchical structure)

---

### Jane App
**Structure**: 4 main sections
- **Preferences** (5 settings)
- **Settings** (18 settings)
- **Offerings** (3 settings)
- **Billing** (10 settings)

**Navigation Style**:
- Clean sidebar with search functionality
- Flat menu structure with category grouping
- Single-page application feel
- Icon-based visual identification

**Complexity**: ★★★★★ (High - most comprehensive, 65+ settings)

---

### CarePatron
**Structure**: 8 main categories
- **My Profile** (4 tabs)
- **Workspace** (5 tabs)
- **Billing** (3 tabs)
- **Insurance** (2 tabs)
- **Scheduling** (3 tabs)
- **Workflows** (2 tabs)
- **Trash** (1 section)
- **Documentation** (1 section)

**Navigation Style**:
- Card-based main hub
- Tab-based navigation within categories
- Modern, spacious design
- Progress bars for usage metrics

**Complexity**: ★★★☆☆ (Moderate - tab-based makes it easy to navigate)

---

### Colib
**Structure**: 2 main sections
- **Personal** (6 settings)
- **Admin** (19 settings)

**Navigation Style**:
- Collapsible Personal/Admin sections
- Two-tier structure
- Emoji-based icons
- Simple, clean hierarchy

**Complexity**: ★★☆☆☆ (Low - simplest structure, clear personal vs. admin separation)
